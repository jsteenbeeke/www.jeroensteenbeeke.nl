Sign Up

Using this page, you can sign up for the mailing list, enabling me to send you regular updates on new and upcoming releases, as well as discounts and contests.

Temporarily disabled

Due to MailChimp going completely apeshit with their pricing scheme the mailing list signups are temporarily disabled.

Can I still opt out after I sign up?

Of course! You will receive an opt-out link after you confirm your e-mail address, as well as with every additional e-mail you receive. And if your e-mail address is not confirmed, you won't need to opt out.

Will you share this information with anyone?

My mailing list is powered by MailChimp, so the information will be shared with them. Aside from that, your e-mail address or personal information will not be shared.
